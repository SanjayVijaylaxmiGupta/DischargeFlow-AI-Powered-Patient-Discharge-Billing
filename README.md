# DischargeFlow-AI-Powered-Patient-Discharge-Billing

DischargeFlow automates hospital patient discharge billing using UiPath Maestro BPMN, AI Agents, and RPA Workflows.

🏥 Problem: Hospital discharge billing takes 2-3 days, involves 6 systems, and has a 12-15% error rate.

⚡ Solution: One Maestro BPMN process orchestrates 2 AI agents + 5 RPA robots to complete the entire discharge-to-payment pipeline in under 2 hours with zero manual effort.

🧩 UiPath Components Used:
• Maestro BPMN — Process orchestration
• ClinicalSummaryAgent — AI discharge summary generation
• ExceptionTriageAgent — AI dispute triage with confidence scoring
• 5 RPA Workflows — Insurance check, billing, payment, data loading, human review

📊 Impact:
• 96% faster (3 days → 2 hours)
• 92% fewer billing errors
